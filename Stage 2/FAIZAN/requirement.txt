List of all the tools use

1)FastQC for quality control (v0.11.9)

2)MultiQC to combine QC files (version 1.12)

3)Fastp to trim the unwanted sequences (0.20.1)

4)BWA for genome indexing and alignment (Version: 0.7.17-r1188)

5)Samtools for working with SAM/BAM files ( 1.13)

6)bcftools for working with vcf (1.13)
